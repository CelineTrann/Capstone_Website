Workout Well With Workout Watcher

Using new smart mat technology that targets your form, your yoga sessions will only improve!

What is the Workout Watcher?

The Workout Watcher is a smart yoga mat that detects the position and pressure of your feet and hands on the mat during different yoga poses. The Workout Watcher calculates the ideal position and pressure for the yoga poses you have chosen and then directs you to correct your form until your accuracy is at least 90%. As you continue to do different poses, the system will keep track of your progress and the percent accuracy over time. The associated mobile app is where you will see your progress being tracked and where you will receive directions to fix your form.

About Us - Who are we?

We are a team of 4 Mechatronics Engineering students from the University of Waterloo working on our fourth-year design project. We have a diverse set of strengths ranging from mechanical, electrical, software, and project management expertise. We have noticed that the pandemic resulted in a lack of physical activity for many individuals, and many are still reluctant to return gyms and group fitness activities. As such, we have Workout Watcher which is a smart yoga mat that guides individuals during their yoga practice without the help of a fitness instructor.
